The Standard About Me

I started this blog on the 22nd of May, 2021, when I was 15 years old. By now, I may or may not be a few years older, depending on how successful this project is. This blog is just for showcasing things I find cool, or things I thought were worth learning. The cool things might be to do with computer science, mathematics, even random competitive programming problem solutions (which I think serve as a good start for that particular algorithm/trick used) or anything in between. Although, moreso than showcasing, I would want this blog to serve the purpose of a photo album. A photo album which would remind me what I learned and did in my (for now) highschool years. I hope it doesn’t turn out to be a photo album that haunts me.

You can acknowledge my existence further on some of these platforms: Instagram, MyAnimeList, Github, Codeforces.
